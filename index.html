WolfQuest's sound analysis by Eduweb: the Roles of Sounds and Images in the Game


In this site I want to explore the use of audio in Eduweb's WolfQuest; Pay careful attention to the loading music, and what emotions it evokes within yourself.:



To me, the loading audio evokes a sense of adventure, and excitement readying myself to embark on an adventure.



Compared to the black and white color scheme and the strange, other-worldly appearance that LIMBO portrayed, WolfQuest's vibrant and life-like graphics used to simulate the natural environment of a wolf does not align with German Expressionism.

In my humble opinion, WolfQuest still carries the expression of emotion that is portrayed in German Expressionism because of the emphasis and careful detail used to craft the art within the game.:
